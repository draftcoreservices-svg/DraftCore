FAQs

Clear boundaries. Clean work. No ambiguity.

Do you provide legal advice or representation?

No. DraftCore provides document drafting and preparatory support only. You should obtain solicitor review/advice before using any document.

Can you work directly with private individuals?

Yes, for straightforward commercial documents (e.g., NDAs, service/consultancy agreements, amendments, and clean-ups). We avoid contentious disputes and anything requiring ongoing advice.

What is the usual turnaround?

Typically 24–72 hours depending on complexity and workload. If you have a deadline, include it in your email and we’ll confirm feasibility.

How do firms use you without disrupting their workflow?

You send a brief (and precedent if you have one). DraftCore delivers a clean first draft in your preferred style. Your solicitor then reviews, advises, and finalises as normal.

How is confidentiality handled?

Discreet handling by default. If you require an NDA before sending documents, say so in your enquiry and we’ll accommodate.
