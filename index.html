FAQs

Clear boundaries. Clean work. No ambiguity.

Do you provide legal advice or representation?

No. DraftCore provides document drafting and preparatory support only. You should obtain solicitor review/advice before using any document.

What is included in a “light revision”?

A light revision covers reasonable drafting adjustments based on your feedback (e.g., wording changes, modest clause tweaks, formatting consistency). Major restructures, new document scopes, or significant additional requirements may be treated as a new instruction.

What is the usual turnaround?

Standard turnaround is typically 48–72 hours depending on complexity and workload. Urgent 24h and 12h options are available for an additional fee (subject to capacity and confirmation).

How do firms use you without disrupting their workflow?

You send a brief (and precedent if you have one). DraftCore delivers a clean first draft in your preferred style. Your solicitor then reviews, advises, and finalises as normal.

How is confidentiality handled?

Discreet handling by default. NDA available on request. Working files are deleted within 21 days of job completion unless you request earlier deletion or ask us to retain materials for ongoing instruction.

Can you provide samples?

Yes — samples can be provided on request in an anonymised/sanitised format.
