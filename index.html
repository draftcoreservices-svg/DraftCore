FAQs

Clear boundaries. Clean work. No ambiguity.

Do you provide legal advice or representation?

No. DraftCore provides document drafting and preparatory support only. You should obtain solicitor review/advice before using any document.

What is the usual turnaround?

Standard turnaround is 48–72 hours depending on complexity and workload. If you need 24h or 12h, expedited fees apply (subject to availability).

Are revisions included?

Yes. Ad-hoc documents include 1 light revision. Retainer documents include 2 light revisions. (A “light revision” means reasonable amends consistent with the original brief, not a change of deal direction.)

How is confidentiality handled?

Discreet handling by default. If you require an NDA before sending documents, say so in your email and we’ll accommodate. Data is deleted within 21 days of job completion unless retention is required for legal, regulatory, or accounting purposes.

Can you provide samples?

Yes — samples can be provided on request (redacted as appropriate). We also work in a format that makes it easy for firms to apply their own branding and finalise internally.

How do firms use you without disrupting their workflow?

You email a brief (and a precedent if you have one). DraftCore delivers a clean first draft aligned to your style. Your solicitor then reviews, advises, and finalises as normal.

How do I instruct DraftCore?

Email hello@draftcore.co.uk with: (1) document type, (2) parties, (3) what the document needs to achieve, (4) deadline, and (5) any precedent or existing draft. We reply with a quote.
