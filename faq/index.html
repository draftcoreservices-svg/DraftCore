FAQs

Clear boundaries. Clean work. No ambiguity.

Do you provide legal advice or representation?

No. DraftCore provides document drafting and preparatory support only. We do not provide legal advice, legal representation, or regulated legal services. Drafts should be reviewed by a qualified solicitor before use.

What is the usual turnaround?

Standard turnaround is 48–72 hours depending on complexity and workload. If you need 24h or 12h, expedited fees apply (subject to availability). We confirm feasibility by email after scope review.

Are revisions included?

Yes. Ad-hoc documents include 1 light revision. Retainer documents include 2 light revisions. A light revision means reasonable changes consistent with the original brief, not a change of deal direction.

Can you provide samples?

Yes — samples can be provided on request (redacted as appropriate). DraftCore does not publish client identities or confidential matter details publicly.
