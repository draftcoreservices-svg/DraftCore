Privacy

Privacy
Policy.

Last updated: January 2025. This policy explains how DraftCore handles your personal data.

Who we are

DraftCore is a UK-based legal drafting support service operating under the trading name DraftCore (draftcore.co.uk). We are not a law firm and do not provide regulated legal services. References to "we", "us", or "DraftCore" refer to the DraftCore service and its operators.

What data we collect

We collect only the personal data you provide directly to us by email. This typically includes:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your organisation or firm name (if applicable)
  • Information you provide in your brief or instructions
  • Documents you send us as part of your project

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics on this website beyond basic hosting-level logs. We do not collect payment data (invoicing is handled via separate third-party tools where applicable).

How we use your data

We use your personal data solely to:

  • Provide the drafting support services you've requested
  • Correspond with you about your project
  • Issue invoices and process payment

We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to your project.

Data retention

Matter documents and client personal data are deleted within 21 days of project completion where practicable. Email correspondence may be retained for a limited administrative period. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@draftcore.co.uk.

Data sharing

We do not share your personal data or matter details with third parties, except where required by law. We do not subcontract your drafting work to third parties.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, email hello@draftcore.co.uk.

Contact

For any privacy-related queries, email hello@draftcore.co.uk.
