Privacy & data handling

Last updated: 30 Jan 2026

DraftCore is confidentiality-first. We use information you provide only to perform the requested drafting work and to communicate about scope, delivery and invoicing.

  • Access: DraftCore does not subcontract drafting work. Access to matter documents is restricted to DraftCore only.
  • Retention: We retain only minimal service records (e.g., company name, fees charged, work category and dates) for operational and accounting purposes.
  • Deletion: Matter documents and client personal data are deleted within 21 days of completion where practicable.
  • Exceptions: We may retain limited information where required for legal, regulatory, tax or accounting reasons, or to resolve a dispute about scope, delivery or payment.
  • Sharing: We do not sell data and do not share your matter documents with third parties.
