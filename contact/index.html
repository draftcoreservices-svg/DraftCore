What to include in your email

For the fastest quote, include: the document type, a brief description of what it needs to do, the parties involved, your deadline, and whether you have an existing precedent or draft to work from.

Confidentiality

All enquiries are handled discreetly. We do not discuss or reference client matter details. An NDA is available on request before any work is shared.

Just ask. If your project is outside our scope, we'll tell you clearly and promptly — no obligation, no awkward back-and-forth.
