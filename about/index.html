About

A boundaries-first
drafting service.

DraftCore is a UK-based, email-only drafting support service built for busy legal workflows. The focus is simple: produce clean, review-ready first drafts so solicitor time stays on review and advice.

The model

What DraftCore is

  • Overflow capacity for routine and repeat drafting
  • Precedent adaptation to match parties and deal terms
  • Clean-up support for messy or inconsistent drafts
  • Confidential by default — NDA available on request
  • UK-based legal support professionals (non-solicitors)
  • Email-only — simple, documented workflow

What DraftCore is not

  • Not a law firm
  • No legal advice or representation
  • No client communication on your behalf
  • No subcontracting of drafting work
  • No regulated legal services
  • Not a replacement for solicitor review
Who we work with

Firms, solicitors,
and individuals.

If you're a firm looking for quiet extra hands that fit neatly into your internal process — start with a single document or a starter retainer. If you're an individual who needs a clean, sensible first draft to take to your solicitor, we can help with that too.

01

Solicitors & firms

Use DraftCore as discreet overflow capacity. Receive clean, formatted first drafts that slot into your internal review process with minimal friction. No branding, no client-facing contact — just the draft.

02

In-house counsel

Speed up routine commercial drafting without taking on headcount. First drafts and precedent adaptation on a per-document basis — scalable to workload.

03

Private individuals

Need a clean starting point for a commercial agreement before taking it to your solicitor? We draft from your brief and hand it to you formatted and structured — ready for professional review.
