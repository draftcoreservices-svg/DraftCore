About

DraftCore is a UK-based, email-only drafting support service built for busy legal workflows. The focus is simple: produce clean, review-ready first drafts and document revisions so solicitor time stays on review and advice.

What DraftCore is

  • Overflow capacity for routine and repeat drafting
  • Precedent adaptation to match parties and deal terms
  • Clean-up support for messy or inconsistent drafts
  • Confidential by default (NDA available on request)

What DraftCore is not

  • Not a law firm
  • No legal advice or representation
  • No client communication on your behalf
  • No subcontracting of drafting work

If you’re a firm and want “quiet extra hands” that fit neatly into your internal process, start with a single document or a starter retainer.
