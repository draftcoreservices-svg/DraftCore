Process

Simple, discreet, and designed to keep your workflow moving.

1) Email the brief

Send context, parties, what the document must achieve, and your deadline. Attach any precedent or existing draft.

2) Quote + timeline

You receive a clear quote and delivery time by email. Standard turnaround is 48–72h (subject to scope).

3) Draft delivered

Delivered as a clean, professionally formatted draft — ready for internal review and finalisation.

Revisions

  • Ad-hoc documents include 1 light revision.
  • Retainers include 2 light revisions per document.
  • Light revision = changes consistent with the original brief.

Scope changes

  • If the deal direction changes materially, scope changes.
  • We confirm revised scope and any fee adjustment before continuing.
  • If a deadline is unrealistic for the scope, you’ll be told quickly.

Faster turnaround can be requested where availability allows — see Pricing.
