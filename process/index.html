Process

Email in.
Quote.
Draft delivered.

The process is deliberately simple. No calls, no portals, no onboarding. Just email with the key details and we'll get started.

How it works
01

Send your brief

Email hello@draftcore.co.uk with the document type, parties, what it needs to do, and your deadline. If you have a precedent or existing draft, attach it. The more context you provide, the tighter the brief — and the better the output.

02

Receive a quote

We'll review your brief and come back with a fixed quote and delivery timeline — usually within a few hours. Quotes are fixed-fee per document. No hourly rates, no surprises.

03

Confirm & we begin

Reply to confirm and we start. No retainer, no onboarding form. Once confirmed, work begins immediately against your deadline.

04

Draft delivered

You receive a polished first draft — clean structure, consistent defined terms, sensible commercial logic, and formatted for easy review. Delivered as a Word document (.docx) ready to mark up.

05

One light revision included

One round of light revisions is included in every quote — covering amendments to structure, terminology, or specific clauses based on your feedback. Substantial re-drafts or scope changes are quoted separately.

What you receive

A draft that's ready to review.

Not a rough skeleton. A formatted, structured first draft with consistent definitions, sensible clause logic, and clean formatting — ready for your mark-up and finalisation.

Format

  • Delivered as Word (.docx)
  • Track-changes-ready formatting
  • Consistent heading styles
  • Numbered clauses and sub-clauses
  • Definitions block where appropriate
  • Clean schedules and annexes

Confidentiality

  • Discreet handling by default
  • NDA available on request
  • No subcontracting of your work
  • Matter docs deleted within 21 days
  • Client data handled per our Privacy Policy
